MCD

8,8,C

 

Displays the MCD (Minor Civil Division) number for the civil township.  Unorganized territories do not have MCD numbers.

 

 

 

Each of the 1789 civil townships has a unique six digit MCD number

 

 

TWP_CODE

10,10,C

 

Displays the township code for the township or unorganized territory.  These codes were developed in the GIM unit to separate civil townships from unorganized territories.

09
